
# PATHFINDER SUPERDOC
## Volume XLV — AI Strategy, Intelligent Automation & Future Decision Engine
**Version:** 1.0 Draft

---

# 1. Purpose

This volume defines how Artificial Intelligence should be incorporated into Pathfinder without compromising deterministic processing, auditability, or customer trust.

AI is intended to assist operators and customers—not replace the core processing engine.

---

# ADR-077 — AI Augments, Never Overrides

**Status:** Accepted

Artificial Intelligence may recommend actions, mappings, classifications, or corrections.

AI shall never silently modify Canonical Orders, product mappings, routing decisions, or destination payloads.

Every AI recommendation must be reviewable, traceable, and optionally accepted before it affects production processing.

---

# 2. AI Design Principles

AI capabilities should be:

- Assistive
- Explainable
- Deterministic after acceptance
- Optional
- Continuously improvable
- Measurable

---

# 3. AI Opportunity Areas

## Customer Onboarding

- Detect likely input template
- Recommend field mappings
- Identify repeated patterns
- Suggest validation profiles

## Product Mapping

- Recommend Unit Numbers
- Detect duplicate mappings
- Identify obsolete mappings
- Suggest aliases

## Validation

- Explain failures in plain language
- Recommend corrective actions
- Detect recurring customer mistakes

## Operations

- Identify unhealthy connectors
- Predict submission failures
- Recommend retry timing
- Surface anomalous processing behavior

---

# 4. AI Recommendation Workflow

```
Raw Input
    ↓
AI Analysis
    ↓
Recommendation
    ↓
Operator Review
    ↓
Accept / Reject
    ↓
Deterministic Processing
```

Accepted recommendations become explicit configuration.

---

# 5. AI Recommendation Objects

Suggested structure:

- recommendation_id
- category
- confidence
- explanation
- suggested_change
- supporting_evidence
- accepted_by
- accepted_at

Recommendations are immutable once recorded.

---

# 6. Prompt Governance

Prompts should be:

- Versioned
- Tested
- Stored separately from application code
- Linked to recommendation history

Prompt changes should be auditable.

---

# ADR-078 — Human Approval for Persistent Changes

Persistent configuration changes generated by AI require explicit approval.

Examples:

- Product mappings
- Template mappings
- Validation rules
- Routes

Temporary recommendations may be displayed without persistence.

---

# 7. Learning Strategy

The platform should improve through accepted operator decisions rather than autonomous behavior.

Accepted mapping corrections become future training examples.

Rejected suggestions should also be retained for evaluation.

---

# 8. AI Metrics

Recommended KPIs:

- Recommendation acceptance rate
- False positive rate
- Mapping accuracy
- Time saved during onboarding
- Validation reduction
- Operator satisfaction

---

# 9. Future AI Modules

Potential services:

- Template Builder Assistant
- Mapping Assistant
- Validation Assistant
- Connector Wizard
- Customer Onboarding Assistant
- Operational Health Advisor

Each should consume Pathfinder APIs rather than bypass platform services.

---

# 10. Success Criteria

AI integration is successful when:

- Customer onboarding time decreases.
- Mapping effort decreases.
- Operators remain in control.
- Canonical Orders remain deterministic.
- Every recommendation is explainable and auditable.
